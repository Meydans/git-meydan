Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us when you use the apps, such as your email address when signing in with Google, and any data you enter into the apps (e.g., grocery items, schedules, vacation plans).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, and to sync your data with your Google Sheets or Google Drive as requested by you.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and/or in your own Google account (Sheets/Drive). We do not store your personal data on our own servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth for authentication and Google APIs (Sheets, Drive) for data storage. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle your data.

5. Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of the apps (e.g., syncing data).

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the repository's issue tracker.
